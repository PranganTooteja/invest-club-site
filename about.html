The Berkeley Investment Group is the first and largest undergraduate investment club at UC Berkeley. Founded in 1997 as the California Investment Association, BIG has consistently beaten the market average with its annual returns and proliferated in capital to over $250,000.

Our Mission

The Berkeley Investment Group's main goal is to educate members on the world of finance and have them feel comfortable investing on their own by the time they graduate.

Our Strategy

BIG's investment strategy borrows from Warren Buffet's principles of value-investing. Every asset in the BIG portfolio is purchased with the belief that it is trading at less than intrinsic value and can be sold at a profit in the future when the market compensates.

Our Partners

The Berkeley Investment Group is thankful for the support of its numerous partners. In the past, BIG analysts have participated in internal case competitions sponsored by elite wealth management firms including Tensile Capital, Alpine Investors, and BlackRock.
