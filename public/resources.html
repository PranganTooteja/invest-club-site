Helpful Articles for Beginners

When learning a new subject or starting a new job, you'll often find acronyms or unfamiliar terminology getting in the way. While some acroynms are avoidable, others are industry standards (think EBIT, Earnings before Interest and Tax). In our experience, when someone doesn't know a term, he or she rarely speaks up and proceeds with the meeting not knowing. That sucks!

We understand why people hesitate to ask, and we want to make sure this happens to beginners less frequently. For those who like to prepare or for those who may be interested, we are currently compiling a list of helpful articles to explain financial terms/concepts you are likely to encounter in our meetings and in the future - and for those who don't mind, you can always ask! Stay Tuned!
